📖 How to Use Kokoro TTS

1. Quick Start

  • Paste or type your text into the big text box.
  • Pick a voice and set the speech speed.
  • Click Generate Audio and wait a few seconds.
  • The audio plays automatically. Use the player controls to pause or replay.

2. Enhance Text (AI-Powered)

Click Enhance Text to let Gemini polish your writing. It adds natural punctuation, fixes pacing, and makes the text flow better for voiceover. The enhanced text is automatically cleaned of any markdown (asterisks, bold, italics) so the TTS engine reads it smoothly.

3. Voice & Speed

  • Voice: Choose between American/British English or Turkish voices. Turkish voices use Edge-TTS; English voices use Kokoro-82M.
  • Speed: Drag the slider from 0.5x (slow) to 1.5x (fast). 1.0x is natural speaking pace.

4. Download

After generation, a Download MP3 button appears. The filename is auto-generated from your text content (e.g., bridge-builder-story.mp3).

5. Tips

  • Maximum text length is 5,000 characters.
  • The first generation after a server cold-start may take ~15 seconds. Subsequent requests are faster.
  • If you see a red error message, check that you entered text and selected a valid voice.
  • Switch language via the top-right dropdown. This changes the UI and available voices.
